Jollibee Group and Manila LGU Empower Senior Citizens and PWDs through Employment Opportunities

Source: Jollibee Foods Corporation

Job Details

Qualifications

  • Must pass screening and be endorsed by Manila PESO

Benefits

Not explicitly mentioned, but likely to include standard benefits offered by Jollibee Group.

Jollibee Group is committed to promoting inclusivity and diversity in the workplace, aiming to provide employment opportunities for senior citizens and PWDs.
